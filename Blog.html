Our Certificate & Online Program

Mar 14, 2023

In an online certification program, students receive certificates through completing non-degree programs at technical schools, colleges, and universities. Certifications for postsecondary courses are quite popular, the reason being they require less time to complete than degrees. Despite busy schedules, students taking online certification programs can access higher education with the utmost ease.


Types of Certification Internal Certification:
Certification provided by the company individual works in
Usually not acceptable outside that organization
Part of Employee Learning and Development and Leadership Development initiatives


External Certification:
Certification provided by a certification body/ vendor
Is valid for use in a specific industry or a particular profession
It needs to be audited to establish certification credibility
Software certification: Individuals are certified on a version of software or hardware.
E.g., SAAS Certification, Oracle certification, Microsoft certification

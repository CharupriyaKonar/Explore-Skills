Adult Learning & Degree Completion

Adult degree completion programs are designed for students who started school but never finished. Colleges are constantly evolving to meet the changing demands of students, so many of whom are adults working to pick up where they left off. It’s now common for schools to accept prior learning credits, which awards credit toward your degree for your previous coursework—and even some job experience.
